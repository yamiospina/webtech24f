All about Yami

Hello & Welcome to Stitch Sisters.

A little about me, growing my mom introduced me to the arts of needle thread crafts.
Starting off with crocheting, I grew up making small projects, like those included on this page.
In college, I have been lucky enough to be a part of Threads & Friends Org. at
Babson College. A special place, where the student body has been introduced to the arts of
needle threading. Now I want to build a safe place for all needle thread crafts enthusiasts, or beginners can join,
to learn and share. So excited to have you all Stitch Sisters!
