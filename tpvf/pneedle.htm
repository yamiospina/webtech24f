What is Punch Needle?

Punch needle is a type of embroidery where you use a special tool called a punch needle to create loops of yarn on fabric. The punch needle pushes the yarn through the fabric, forming loops on the other side. This technique creates a textured, raised design that looks a bit like a rug.
