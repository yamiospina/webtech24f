what is knitting?

Knitting is a craft where you use two needles to create fabric from yarn. By looping the yarn around the needles and pulling it through previous loops, you form stitches that build up into patterns and shapes. It's a technique used to make items like sweaters, scarves, hats,etc. Knitting can be both relaxing and creative, allowing you to make cozy, handmade pieces. üß∂‚ú®
