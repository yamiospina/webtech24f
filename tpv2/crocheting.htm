What is crocheting?

Crocheting is a craft where you use a hooked needle(crochet hook) to create fabric from yarn or thread. You make loops and pull them through other loops to form stitches, which then build up into patterns and shapes. It's a bit like knitting, but with just one hook instead of two needles. It's great for making things like blankets, scarves, hats, and even toys!
