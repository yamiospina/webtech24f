All about Yami

Hello & Welcome to Stitch Sisters. A little about me, growing my mom introduced me to the arts of needle thread crafts. Starting off with crohceting, I grew up making small projects, like those included on this page. In college, I have been lucky enough to be apart of Threads & Friends Org. at Babson College. A especial place, where the student body have been introduced to the arts of neddle threading. Now I want to build a safe place for all needle thread crafts enthuasists, or beginners can join, to learn and share . So excited to have you all Stitch Sisters !
